# Anki: which deck

**Recommendation: Gregmat AnkiWeb deck `962516846`** (~868 unique words, all of Gregmat's vocab groups + synonyms, free).

## Why this one

Your existing tracker is already built around the Gregmat ecosystem — Prepswift videos, Quant Mountain, Vocab Mountain, Gregmat groups. The daily plan references "Gregmat groups" explicitly. Picking any other deck means re-mapping all those references, and the daily vocab counts (110 → 175 words per week) are calibrated for this deck specifically.

Reddit's r/GRE consensus over the last 3 years has been remarkably stable: **if you're on a Gregmat study path, use the Gregmat deck.** Magoosh's flashcards are also excellent but they overlap heavily and live inside Magoosh's own app, which fragments your workflow.

## Install (5 min)

1. Install Anki desktop (free): [apps.ankiweb.net](https://apps.ankiweb.net). Use it as your source-of-truth — the mobile app syncs to it.
   - **iOS:** AnkiMobile, $25 one-time (supports the developer). Worth it.
   - **Android:** AnkiDroid, free.
2. Create a free AnkiWeb account at [ankiweb.net](https://ankiweb.net). Sign into it on both desktop and mobile so they sync.
3. On desktop, **File → Download Shared Deck** (or go to [ankiweb.net/shared/info/962516846](https://ankiweb.net/shared/info/962516846) and click "Download"). Import the `.apkg`.
4. Tools → **Preferences → Review**: bump "New cards/day" to 20 (matches Week 1 target). Bump "Maximum reviews/day" to 200 to handle the build-up.
5. Sync. Open on phone. You're done.

## Daily routine (matches the tracker)

- **Morning, 5 min:** Review yesterday's reps. Don't suspend cards on day 1 — let the algorithm rank what's hard.
- **Lunch, 15 min:** 15 reviews. This is in your daily plan as "Anki lunch reps."
- **Evening, 15-20 min:** 15-20 new words. This is in your daily plan as "Anki: N new words."
- **Sunday, 10 min:** Filter by **leech** (cards you keep failing). Drill those manually. This is part of the Sunday review cycle.

868 words ÷ ~110 new/week pace = ~8 weeks to finish (matches your Foundation + Strategy phases). If you're flying through it by Week 7, install the supplemental deck below.

## If you finish early

**Gregmat supplemental: AnkiWeb deck `125581439`** (~600 additional words). It's curated as the unique-words diff between Gregmat and the Magoosh/PrepScholar/PowerScore lists. Only install this after finishing the main deck — otherwise you're just adding noise.

## What NOT to do

- Don't run Gregmat + Magoosh + Manhattan decks simultaneously. The overlap is huge (~60-70%), and you'll burn out reviewing the same words 3 ways.
- Don't make your own deck. Sounds productive; it's procrastination.
- Don't bury yourself by setting "new cards/day" to 50 in Week 1. The Anki review load 2 weeks later will crush you when actual study hours peak in Week 8 (PTO intensive). Stay at 20-25/day until Week 6, then taper to maintenance.

## Tracking it

In the app's Daily panel, the vocab tasks live alongside everything else and your `actual_minutes` field lets you log if Anki took longer than the planned 15-20 min. If you're consistently over by 50%, that's a signal to reduce daily new-card count by 5. Don't let Anki eat your practice time.
